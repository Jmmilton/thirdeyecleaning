Our Services

  • $110** for up to 3 hours. $30**/hr for each additional hour
  • One hour service only: $60**
  • **plus tax

Service includes a thorough cleaning & disinfecting of everything listed below:

Kitchen:
  • Wipe down small appliances
  • Wipe inside and outside of microwave
  • Wipe down cupboard doors & backsplash
  • Wipe outside of fridge, oven, dishwasher
  • Wipe down countertops, behind and under small appliances
  • Wipe down windows, baseboards, floor, door handles, light switches
  • Dust frames, shelves, light fixtures
  • Empty Garbage & recycling
Bathrooms:
  • Clean sink, vanity and mirrors
  • Clean inside and outside toilet
  • Scrub bathtub, tiles & grout
  • Clean shower doors
  • Dust frames, shelves, light fixtures
  • Empty Garbage
  • Wipe down windows, baseboards, floor, door handles, light switches
Living Areas & Bedrooms
  • Tidy up / organize small objects & clothing
  • Make or change beds
  • Dust frames, shelves, light fixtures
  • Dust furniture
  • Vacuum upholstered furniture
  • Wipe down windows, baseboards, floors, door handles, light switches
  • Empty trash bins

Please Note...

An initial visit to the home before the service begins is required in order to assess which type of service will be needed and at which rate.
***DURING COVID-19 we will be conducting these visits via video chat over Facetime or Zoom.***

We do not carry our own cleaning supplies. We use what you already have at home. Natural cleaning products are preferred and recommended for a toxic-free environment :) We try to minimize our use of paper towels, so reusable cleaning cloths are recommended.
***DURING COVID-19 we will be providing hydrogen peroxide disinfectant which will be used on all surfaces and touch points after they have been cleaned. Using the products and supplies you have at your home limits the possibility of spreading any potential virus to other homes or businesses.***

We pride ourselves on being flexible and working with you to provide a service you will be more than happy with. We are open to tailoring the service to your needs. If there are any tasks not listed above that you’d like us to do, or if there’s anything we should leave out, please let us know.

We DO NOT service: Homes with any pest infestations (rodents, roaches, bedbugs, etc.). Homes with high mold content. Homes with excessive hoarding.
